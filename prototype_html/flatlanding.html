Flatlanding

A transdimensional adventure prototype

Short Game Prototype that explores 3D gameplay blending with 2D. Made with Unity as part of a gamejam in 1 week with a team of 4. During this, I led the team as game lead, solo-programmed the game in Unity and designing the concept of the game with the prompt "impossible spaces". Here I explore a game where you play as a astronaut crash landing on a planet inhabited by... 2D citizens?
