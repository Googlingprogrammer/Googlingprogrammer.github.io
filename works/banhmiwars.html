Banh Mi Wars

Card Game

Banh Mi Wars is a 2-4 players competitive card game about making a delicious Banh Mi sandwich - as cards! Players are competing Banh Mi vendors trying to be the first to assemble an order. Draw the required pieces, cook the needed ingredients in your stove, overcome the many random events, fight and sabotage your way in all out BANH MI WARS!
